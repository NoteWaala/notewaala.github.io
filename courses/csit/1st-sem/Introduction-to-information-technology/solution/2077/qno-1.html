1.

Differentiate analog computer with digital computer. What is CPU and how its works? What does an instruction cycle in CPU consists of?

Ans

The difference between analog and digital computer are:

S.N Analog computer Digital compute
1. It works with continuous values, i.e they process continuous data. They work with discrete values, i.e they process discrete data.
2. The speed of analog computers is less than that of digital computers. The speed of digital computers is more in comparison to analog computers.
3. It has very less memory, and can store limited amount of data. It has more memory space, hence stores large amounts of data.
4. It doesn’t have any state. It has two steps: On and Off.
5. They are not as reliable as digital computers. It is more reliable.
6. It doesn’t provide performance as good as digital computers. It provides great performance.
7. Its processing speed is low. Its processing speed is high.
8. It depends on physical variations. It doesn’t depend on physical variations.
9. The results given by analog computers have very less accuracy. They are easy to use.
10. They are not easy to use. The results are more accurate.
11. They have a complex architecture. Their architecture is not as complex as analog computers.
12. It has low readability. They have high readability.
13. The results of analog computers are given out as voltage signals. The results are displayed on the monitor/display screen.
14. They use analog encoding. They use digital encoding technique.
15. They use special purpose devices. They consume less power.
16. They consume more power. It can be used as a general purpose device.
17. Examples includes analog clock and thermometer. Examples include laptop, digital camera, digital watches >
