Course Discription & Objective
Course Discription:

The course contains concepts and techniques of linear algebra. The course topics include systems of linear equations, determinants, vectors and vector spaces, eigenvalues and eigenvectors and singular value decomposition of matrix.


Course Objectives:

The main objective of the course is to make students familiar with the concepts and techniques in linear algebra,, solve system of linear equations with Gauss-Jordan method, to impart knowledge of vector space and subspace, eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a matrix and get the idea of diagonalization of a matrix, linear programming, Group, Rings and Fields.
